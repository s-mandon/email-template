Part Exchange?

Please remember to bring with you the following:

  • Current registration document (V5c)
    (IMPORTANT) - By law we cannot accept a part exchange without a current V5c.
  • MOT certificate (if applicable)
  • Service history
  • All keys
  • Locking wheel nut key

Your sales executive will complete an online notification to DVLA to advise of the transfer of the part exchange vehicle to us. Please do not contact DVLA yourself prior to bringing in your car to us.

Our Heritage

Haynes of Maidstone is one of the very few family companies in the UK that has been in business over 200 years. Therefore, you can be assured that your vehicle comes with a wealth of history and a multi award winning team behind it. To celebrate this history, we have produced a quality enamel badge to display on your car, which yourself or one of the team can affix for you.

Customers who proudly display this badge on their car will, in return, receive a 10% discount on any parts or accessories purchased from us. If this is of interest, please ask your sales executive for more details.
